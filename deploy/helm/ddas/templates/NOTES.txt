Migrations apply automatically at boot. First boot creates the admin from
admin.email / admin.password (only if no admin exists yet). Both are safe
under replicas > 1: migrations and the bootstrap take advisory locks.
{{- if gt (int .Values.replicaCount) 1 }}

HA mode: {{ .Values.replicaCount }} replicas over one Postgres. Sessions,
rate limits, jobs, and webhook delivery are shared through the database;
blobs MUST be on shared storage (env.DDAS_BLOB_DRIVER=s3 or an RWX volume —
the chart refused to render otherwise). Details: docs/ha.md.
{{- else }}

Scaling out later: set replicaCount > 1 AND move blobs to shared storage
(env.DDAS_BLOB_DRIVER=s3 recommended). See docs/ha.md.
{{- end }}

Remember: export an audit checkpoint (GET /api/v1/audit/checkpoint) and store
it OUTSIDE the cluster — that is your defense against database-level history
rewrites.
