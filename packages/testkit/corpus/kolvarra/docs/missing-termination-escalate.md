# Supply Agreement KV-SA-2026-0233

**Buyer (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Marconilaan 4, Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Supplier:** Duvenhoek Bearings B.V., Lagedijk 31, 5705 BX Helmond, the Netherlands

Signed at Eindhoven on 15 June 2026.

## 1. Subject

The Supplier will supply sealed spherical roller bearings and matched sleeve assemblies for the KX-7 and KX-9 pump lines, to the Kolvarra drawing revisions listed in Schedule A, delivered to the Eindhoven plant against monthly call-offs. The purchase was tendered and awarded as a standard procurement under procedure PROC-STD-04.

## 2. Price and payment

The agreed contract value is EUR 95,000 (ninety-five thousand euro) at the fixed unit prices in Schedule B, payable net 45 days from invoice.

## 3. Quality

Goods are manufactured under the Supplier's ISO 9001 certified system; PPAP level 3 documentation accompanies the first delivery of each line item.

## 4. Liability

The Supplier's aggregate liability under this agreement is capped at twice the contract value, save for wilful misconduct.

## 5. General

Both parties are established in the Netherlands and all deliveries are domestic; there is no cross-border element to the supply. The supply of catalogue bearings is not a regulated activity and requires no permit or approval. Dun & Bradstreet reports the Supplier at the equivalent of BBB+ on the international rating scale. No announcement or external communication will be made regarding this agreement; it has no external visibility. Internally the agreement concerns the Purchasing, Incoming Quality, and Assembly Engineering functions.
