# Supply Agreement KV-SA-2026-0288

**Buyer (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Supplier:** Hensbroek Polymer Seals B.V., Nijverheidsweg 14, 3812 PK Amersfoort, the Netherlands

Date: 24 June 2026
Award route: tender concluded as a standard procurement under procedure PROC-STD-04.

## 1. Subject

Supply of elastomer lip seals and O-ring kits for the KX-7 pump line to the specifications in Schedule 1, delivered to the Eindhoven plant against weekly call-offs.

## 2. Price and payment

The committed contract value is EUR 210,000 (two hundred ten thousand euro) over the full term at fixed unit prices, payable net 45 days from invoice.

## 3. Term and review

The agreement has a fixed term of thirty-six (36) months from the effective date. The parties acknowledge that market conditions may evolve, and agree that either party may revisit the arrangement in good faith at the annual business review, where the parties will discuss volumes, pricing, and service performance for the coming year.

## 4. Liability

The Supplier's aggregate liability under this agreement is capped at 100% of the committed contract value, save for wilful misconduct.

## 5. General

Both parties are established in the Netherlands; deliveries are domestic with no cross-border element. The supply of catalogue sealing components is not a regulated activity and requires no permit. Graydon reports the Supplier at the equivalent of BBB on the international rating scale. No external communication is planned; the agreement has no external visibility. Use of the parts is confined to the Eindhoven Assembly team.
