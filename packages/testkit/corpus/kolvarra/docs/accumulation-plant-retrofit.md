# Contract Award Memo — Poznań Plant Energy Retrofit KV-CAP-2026-0021

**Employer (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands, acting for its Poznań production plant
**Contractor:** Zelenko Industrial Contracting S.A., ul. Przemysłowa 77, 61-541 Poznań, Poland

Date: 4 June 2026
Sourcing route: awarded after a three-bidder tender; the award is a standard procurement of construction and installation works under procedure PROC-STD-04.

## 1. Scope of works

Deep energy retrofit of the Poznań plant: replacement of the compressed-air plant, heat recovery on the test benches, roof-mounted photovoltaics, and a new building-management system, executed in phases over the running plant.

## 2. Contract price

The contract price is EUR 2,400,000 (two million four hundred thousand euro), fixed lump sum, payable against certified milestone completion.

## 3. Term and termination

The works are programmed over fourteen (14) months from site mobilization. The Employer may terminate the contract for convenience at any time against payment for works executed and demonstrable demobilization costs.

## 4. Liability

The Contractor's aggregate liability is capped at 100% of the contract price, excluding only wilful misconduct and death or personal injury.

## 5. Compliance

The works proceed under the plant's existing building and environmental consents; no new permit or regulatory approval is required, and the retrofit is not a regulated activity. All works are performed within Poland by the Polish contractor for the Polish plant; the engagement is domestic to the plant's jurisdiction with no cross-border performance.

## 6. Visibility and stakeholders

The retrofit is co-funded under a national industrial decarbonization programme and will be publicly announced; coverage in national Polish business press is anticipated at the signing ceremony with the programme agency. The project affects an ecosystem of stakeholders around the plant: component suppliers whose deliveries are re-sequenced, customers whose orders are re-phased during changeover windows, the municipal utility, the grid operator, and the plant workforce.

## 7. Contractor standing

Zelenko Industrial Contracting S.A. is rated BBB- on the international scale by a recognized credit agency and has completed two prior works packages for the Poznań plant on schedule.
