# Tooling Supply Contract KV-TL-2026-0114

**Purchaser (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Toolmaker:** Aldervik Metals GmbH, Werkstrasse 19, 73431 Aalen, Germany

Date: 12 May 2026
Sourcing route: awarded following a two-round tender; the award is a standard procurement of production tooling under procedure PROC-STD-04.

## 1. Scope

Design, build, and try-out of a progressive die set and two casting core packages for the KX-11 pump housing, including first-article inspection at the Toolmaker's works and installation support at the Poznań plant.

## 2. Price

The fixed contract price is EUR 265,000 (two hundred sixty-five thousand euro), payable 30/60/10 against milestones (order, try-out acceptance, final documentation).

## 3. Term and termination

The contract runs for nine (9) months from the order date through final acceptance. The Purchaser may terminate for convenience at any stage on fourteen (14) days' written notice against payment of documented costs incurred plus a 5% cancellation fee on the unperformed balance.

## 4. Liability

Given the single-source criticality of the tooling to the KX-11 launch, the parties have expressly agreed that no limitation of liability shall apply to either party under this contract; each party remains liable for the full extent of damages recoverable at law.

## 5. Compliance and logistics

Tool steel and machining services under this contract are not a regulated activity; no export control listing applies to the die set. The finished tooling ships cross-border from Aalen, Germany, to the Poznań plant.

## 6. Standing and internal impact

Creditreform reports Aldervik Metals GmbH at the equivalent of BBB- on the international rating scale. There is no external visibility: no announcement, publication, or customer communication accompanies this award. Internally the project involves the Tooling, Manufacturing Engineering, and Purchasing departments across two plants.
