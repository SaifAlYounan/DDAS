# Board Memorandum — Proposed Pilot Joint Venture with Stromvik Hydraulik AB

To: Executive Board, Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V.
From: R. Meulendijk, Director of Business Development
Date: 26 May 2026

## 1. Proposal

Business Development proposes that Kolvarra, as party of the first part, enter a pilot joint venture with Stromvik Hydraulik AB of Gothenburg, Sweden, to co-develop and market a containerized dewatering pump skid for the Nordic mining sector. Kolvarra has never entered into a venture of this kind; the proposed action is a first-of-kind joint venture formation with no matching precedent in the contract register.

## 2. Financial commitment

Kolvarra's total committed contribution to the pilot vehicle is EUR 380,000 (three hundred eighty thousand euro), covering cash capital of EUR 300,000 and in-kind engineering hours valued at EUR 80,000. No further funding obligation arises without a new board decision.

## 3. Structure, term, and exit

The pilot phase runs for eighteen (18) months from formation. Either party may withdraw from the venture for convenience on ninety (90) days' written notice during the pilot phase, in which case the vehicle is wound up and remaining cash is returned pro rata.

## 4. Risk terms

The draft joint venture agreement caps each party's liability to the other at the amount of its committed contribution. Stromvik Hydraulik AB carries a Standard & Poor's long-term rating of BBB. The venture's activity — assembly and sale of dewatering skids — is not a regulated activity and requires no licence in the target markets. Operations will be cross-border by design: engineering in Eindhoven, assembly in Gothenburg.

## 5. Visibility and stakeholders

Launch communication is limited to industry channels: a joint booth at Euro Mine Expo and coverage in the mining trade press. The venture involves external parties beyond the two shareholders, including mining customers, a Swedish assembly subcontractor, and a shared distributor network.

## 6. Recommendation

Approve entry into the pilot joint venture on the terms above, subject to final legal review of the shareholders' agreement.
