# Procurement Approval Request KV-PR-2026-0592

**Requesting entity (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Proposed supplier:** Vosskamp Precision Castings GmbH, Giessereistrasse 8, 44805 Bochum, Germany

Date: 21 April 2026
Sourcing route: competitive three-quote tender; this request is a standard procurement of production materials under procedure PROC-STD-04.

## 1. Subject

Supply of grey-iron volute casings and bearing pedestals for the KX-7 pump family, machined to drawing, delivered to the Eindhoven plant on a rolling monthly call-off.

## 2. Value

The evaluated contract value is EUR 340,000 (three hundred forty thousand euro) over the supply period, at fixed unit prices per the tender schedule. Payment net 60 days from invoice.

## 3. Term and exit

The supply period is twelve (12) months from first call-off. Kolvarra may terminate the contract for convenience at any time on thirty (30) days' written notice, paying only for goods delivered and conforming work in progress.

## 4. Risk terms

The draft contract caps the supplier's aggregate liability at 100% of the contract value, with the customary carve-outs for wilful misconduct. Creditreform assigns Vosskamp a rating equivalent to BBB on the international scale. The goods are ordinary industrial castings: their supply is not a regulated activity and no export licence, permit, or approval applies. Deliveries move cross-border from Bochum, Germany, to Eindhoven, the Netherlands.

## 5. Visibility and internal impact

No external communication is planned or expected; the award has no external visibility. Internally the change affects the Procurement, Engineering, and Quality functions, which jointly reviewed the tender scores.
