# Framework Supply and Integration Agreement KV-FW-2026-0301

**Customer (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Supplier:** Merkato Fluid Systems S.r.l., Via Stradella 44, 10147 Turin, Italy

Date: 22 June 2026
Award route: negotiated award recorded as a standard procurement under procedure PROC-STD-04. This main body is executed together with Annexes A through C, which form an integral part of the agreement; committed purchase volumes are set out exclusively in Annex B.

## 1. Subject

The Supplier will integrate its mechanical-seal cartridges into the KX-11 pump line and supply production quantities against call-offs, per the technical specification in Annex A.

## 2. Integration fee

For the integration and qualification work-package the Customer pays a one-time integration fee of EUR 180,000 (one hundred eighty thousand euro), invoiced 50% at kick-off and 50% at qualification sign-off. Production pricing and committed purchase volumes are exclusively those stated in Annex B.

## 3. Term and termination

The agreement runs for twenty-four (24) months from the effective date. The Customer may terminate the agreement for convenience on sixty (60) days' written notice, against payment for work performed and non-cancellable commitments.

## 4. Liability

Each party's aggregate liability under the agreement is capped at EUR 500,000 per event and in the aggregate, save for wilful misconduct.

## 5. Compliance and logistics

Seal cartridges are ordinary industrial components; their supply is not a regulated activity and no licence or approval is required. Deliveries are cross-border from Turin, Italy, to Eindhoven and Poznań.

## 6. Standing, visibility, and internal impact

Cerved assigns the Supplier the equivalent of BBB on the international rating scale. No announcement or other external communication accompanies this agreement; it has no external visibility. Internally the programme involves the Purchasing, Design Engineering, and Quality functions.
