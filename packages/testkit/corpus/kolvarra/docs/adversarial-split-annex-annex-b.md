# Annex B to Framework Supply and Integration Agreement KV-FW-2026-0301 — Commercial Terms

This Annex B forms an integral part of the Framework Supply and Integration Agreement dated 22 June 2026 between Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V. and Merkato Fluid Systems S.r.l. In case of conflict on commercial volumes, this Annex prevails over the main body.

## B.1 Committed volumes

For each of the two contract years of the term, the Customer commits to a minimum committed order volume of EUR 250,000 (two hundred fifty thousand euro) per contract year of production seal cartridges at the unit prices in table B-2. The commitment applies to each contract year separately and is not transferable between contract years.

## B.2 Unit prices

Unit prices are fixed for the term per table B-2 (attached), FCA Turin (Incoterms 2020).

## B.3 Invoicing

Production deliveries are invoiced monthly, net 60 days. The integration fee under clause 2 of the main body is invoiced separately and does not count toward the minimum committed order volume.
