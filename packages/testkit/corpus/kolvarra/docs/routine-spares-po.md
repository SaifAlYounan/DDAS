# Purchase Order KV-PO-2026-04471

**Buyer (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Marconilaan 4, 5621 AB Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Supplier:** Bremvik Forge Sp. z o.o., ul. Odlewnicza 12, 61-315 Poznań, Poland

Date of issue: 14 March 2026
Reference: framework quality agreement KV-QA-2019-011; Kolvarra standard purchasing conditions rev. 7.

## 1. Scope of order

This purchase order covers replacement spare parts for the Kolvarra KX-9 pump line: forty (40) forged impeller blanks (part no. KX9-IMP-40), sixty (60) shaft sleeves (part no. KX9-SLV-22), and associated gasket sets, for stock replenishment at the Eindhoven service warehouse. The order is a routine spare-parts purchase order under the existing framework quality agreement.

## 2. Price and payment

The total order value is EUR 18,500 (eighteen thousand five hundred euro), fixed, DAP Eindhoven (Incoterms 2020), payable net 45 days from receipt of a conforming invoice. No further amounts are committed under this order.

## 3. Delivery

Delivery in one lot no later than 30 April 2026. Cross-border shipment from the Supplier's Poznań works to the Buyer's Eindhoven warehouse; the Supplier arranges carriage.

## 4. Term and termination

This order remains in effect for a term of twelve (12) months from the date of issue to cover warranty returns. The Buyer may terminate this order for convenience at any time on written notice, against payment for conforming goods already delivered.

## 5. Liability

The Supplier's aggregate liability under this order is capped at the total order value. Nothing in this order excludes liability for wilful misconduct.

## 6. Miscellaneous

Vendor file note: Bremvik Forge Sp. z o.o. carries a Standard & Poor's long-term issuer rating of BBB+. The parties record that the supply of pump spare parts is not a regulated activity and requires no permit, licence, or regulatory approval on either side. This order concerns internal stock replenishment only, with no external visibility and no announcement of any kind. Operational impact is confined to the Maintenance & Reliability team at the Eindhoven plant.
