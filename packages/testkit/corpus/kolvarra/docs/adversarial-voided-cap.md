# Renewal and Amendment — Automation Support Services Agreement KV-AS-2022-009

**Customer (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Provider:** Corvatch Automation AG, Bahnhofstrasse 88, 8001 Zurich, Switzerland

Date: 27 June 2026

## Part I — Renewal

1. This instrument records the renewal of the Automation Support Services Agreement dated 1 September 2022, under which the Provider maintains the PLC and SCADA layer of the Eindhoven and Poznań plants. The renewal restates the agreement as sections 1 through 12 below and simultaneously amends it per Part II.

2. The committed value for the renewal term is EUR 310,000 (three hundred ten thousand euro), invoiced monthly in arrears.

3. The renewal term is twenty-four (24) months from 1 August 2026. The Customer may terminate for convenience on sixty (60) days' written notice.

4.-8. [Operational sections restated without change: service levels, response times, spare-part handling, site access, and data exchange.]

9. Limitation of liability. Subject to section 10 (exclusions), each party's aggregate liability arising under or in connection with this agreement is capped at 150% of the fees paid or payable in the twelve months preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

10. The cap in section 9 does not apply to death, personal injury, or wilful misconduct.

11. The services are ordinary industrial maintenance services; they are not a regulated activity and require no permit or approval in either country. The services are performed cross-border by the Provider's Zurich engineering desk with on-site interventions at both plants.

12. No announcement or external communication will accompany this renewal; it has no external visibility. Internally the renewal concerns the Maintenance, Controls Engineering, and IT/OT Security functions. Bisnode reports the Provider at the equivalent of BBB+ on the international rating scale.

## Part II — Amendment No. 1

A1. In consideration of the Provider's revised pricing in Part I, the parties agree that section 9 (Limitation of liability) is deleted and disapplied in its entirety with effect from the start of the renewal term; no limitation of liability shall apply to either party under the renewed agreement, and each party is liable for the full extent of damages recoverable at law. Section 10 is amended accordingly and survives only as a clarification of non-excludable liabilities.

A2. Except as amended by this Part II, all sections of Part I remain in full force.
