# Services Agreement KV-SV-2026-0187

**Customer (party of the first part):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Provider:** Brenholt Analytics ApS, Store Strandstraede 9, 1255 Copenhagen, Denmark

Date: 18 June 2026
Award route: selected by three-quote comparison as a standard procurement of professional services under procedure PROC-STD-04.

## 1. Services

The Provider will operate its vibration-analytics platform on telemetry streamed from the KX-9 test benches, deliver monthly anomaly reports, and maintain the trained models for the bench fleet. Work is performed remotely from the Provider's Copenhagen office against data delivered cross-border from the Eindhoven test facility to Denmark.

## 2. Fees

The total fee for the services is EUR 130,000 (one hundred thirty thousand euro) for the full service period, invoiced quarterly in advance, net 30 days.

## 3. Term and termination

The service period is twelve (12) months from the start date. The Customer may terminate for convenience at any time on thirty (30) days' written notice, with prepaid fees for unstarted quarters refunded.

## 4. Liability

The Provider's aggregate liability is capped at the total fees paid under this agreement.

## 5. Communication and internal use

Neither party will make any announcement concerning this agreement; the engagement has no external visibility. The reports are used solely by the Test Engineering team at the Eindhoven bench facility.
