# Renewal Memorandum — Distribution and Aftermarket Services Agreement KV-DST-2020-007

**Party of the first part:** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Party of the second part (Distributor):** Parsian Flow Equipment Co., No. 41 Valiasr Avenue, Tehran, Iran

Date: 9 May 2026

## 1. Renewal

This memorandum records the renewal of the Distribution and Aftermarket Services Agreement dated 3 August 2020, under which the Distributor resells Kolvarra KX-series pumps and provides first-line aftermarket service in its home market. Commercial terms are unchanged except as stated below.

## 2. Committed volumes

The Distributor commits to minimum purchases of EUR 420,000 (four hundred twenty thousand euro) in aggregate over the renewal term, at the list prices in Schedule 1, payable by confirmed irrevocable letter of credit before shipment.

## 3. Term and termination

The renewal term is twelve (12) months from 1 June 2026. Either party may terminate the agreement for convenience on sixty (60) days' written notice.

## 4. Liability

The aggregate liability of each party under the renewed agreement is capped at the value of purchases in the six months preceding the claim.

## 5. Nature of goods and shipments

The distributed goods are standard industrial water and slurry pumps; their sale and distribution is not a regulated activity in the sense of Kolvarra's compliance manual. All shipments are cross-border from the Eindhoven or Poznań plants to the Distributor's warehouse.

## 6. Visibility and stakeholders

The renewal will be announced through the Distributor's trade-channel newsletter and at the regional industry fair, with visibility limited to industry channels. Performance of the agreement involves external parties: the Distributor, its sub-dealers, and end customers in its territory.
