# Renewal Memorandum — Master Services Agreement KV-MSA-2021-003

**Party of the first part (Customer):** Kolvarra Industrial Systems B.V., Eindhoven, the Netherlands
**Party of the second part (Supplier):** Halcyon Drivetrain Components Inc., 2200 Harbor Point Drive, Wilmington, Delaware, United States

Date: 2 April 2026

## 1. Renewal

This memorandum records the renewal of the Master Services Agreement dated 15 June 2021 (KV-MSA-2021-003) for machining and heat-treatment services on drivetrain housings supplied to Kolvarra's Eindhoven assembly line. All terms of the 2021 agreement continue except as stated below.

## 2. Committed value and payment

Over the renewal term the Customer commits to a total contract value of USD 2,000,000 (two million United States dollars), invoiced monthly against released work orders, payable net 60 days. Prices are fixed in USD for the full renewal term.

## 3. Term and termination

The renewal term is twenty-four (24) months from 1 July 2026. Either party may terminate for convenience on ninety (90) days' written notice, without penalty, subject to payment for services performed to the effective date of termination.

## 4. Liability

Clause 14 of the 2021 agreement remains in force: each party's aggregate liability is capped at the fees paid in the twelve months preceding the claim, with the customary carve-outs for wilful misconduct.

## 5. Compliance and visibility

The services are ordinary commercial machining services; neither party's performance constitutes a regulated activity, and no permit or regulatory approval is required. Deliveries are made cross-border from the Supplier's Delaware facility to the Netherlands. The renewal involves no press release or external communication of any kind. Internally, the renewal affects the Procurement, Engineering, and Quality functions, each of which has countersigned this memorandum.

## 6. Counterparty standing

The Supplier carries a Fitch long-term issuer default rating of A-, affirmed 11 February 2026, and has met all contractual service levels during the current term.
